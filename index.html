About Me

I'm Sohaib Younas, a creative web designer and SEO specialist based in Sargodha, Pakistan, with 4+ years of experience building responsive, SEO-friendly websites for clients worldwide. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and specialize in custom coding with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, along with WordPress development using Elementor Pro and WooCommerce, Wix, and Shopify.
Over the years, I have delivered 145+ projects for 100+ clients across eCommerce, business, and educational sectors, working through Upwork, Fiverr, and LinkedIn. My approach combines clean code, modern design, and proven SEO strategies to help businesses rank higher on Google and convert more visitors into customers.
Alongside my freelance career, I work as a Police Station Assistant (PSA) in Punjab Police, managing data entry and administrative record-keeping. This role has sharpened my discipline, accuracy, and time management, qualities I bring to every web design and SEO project.
Whether you need a custom-coded website, a WordPress or Shopify store, or full SEO optimization to grow your organic traffic, I'm ready to turn your idea into a fast, responsive, and search-friendly website.
